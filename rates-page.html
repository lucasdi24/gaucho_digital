Before you book

Reservation & Policies

Deposit & Confirmation

A non-refundable deposit of 50% is required at booking. Without it, your reservation is not held. Contact us in advance if you anticipate payment delays — we may offer a short extension at our discretion.

Final Payment

The remaining balance must be paid no later than 90 days before your scheduled arrival. This final payment, like the deposit, is non-refundable.

Date Changes

Date changes within the same season are allowed, subject to availability. Transfers to a different season require forfeiting your deposit and placing a new one. All changes must be coordinated in advance.

Travel Insurance

We strongly recommend purchasing travel insurance. Since deposits and final payments are non-refundable, insurance protects you against illness, weather disruptions, or other unforeseen circumstances.

Travel information

Getting to Rio Manso's Operations

EZE — Ezeiza International AEP — Aeroparque Jorge Newbery BRC — Bariloche Airport

The nearest airport to Rio Manso Lodge is in San Carlos de Bariloche. Flights connect via Buenos Aires — if you have time, we recommend spending a couple of days in the city.

International flights arrive at Ezeiza (EZE); most domestic flights depart from Aeroparque (AEP). Check if a direct EZE→BRC flight is available — Aerolineas Argentinas often operates this route. New connections from Santiago, Chile are also being added. Allow at least 4–5 hours between your EZE arrival and AEP departure.

Transfer tip

Use a reputable transfer service between Ezeiza and Aeroparque — we recommend Manuel Tienda León (counter after baggage claim). A private car costs ~$60 for the one-hour ride, no reservation needed.

The transfer from Rio Manso Lodge to El Atravesado Lodge is a scenic 5-hour drive. Let us know if you'd like to combine both lodges.

EZE — Ezeiza International AEP — Aeroparque Jorge Newbery EQS — Esquel Airport

The nearest airport to El Atravesado Lodge is in Esquel (EQS), with regular flights from Buenos Aires. Note: there are currently no flights to Esquel on Tuesdays, so plan accordingly.

International flights arrive at Ezeiza (EZE); domestic flights to Esquel depart from Aeroparque (AEP). Allow at least 4–5 hours between connections. Once in Esquel, we'll arrange your transfer to the lodge — approximately 2 hours through beautiful Patagonian scenery.

Need to arrive early?

If flight schedules require an overnight in Esquel, we're happy to recommend hotels in the area. Just let us know — we'll be glad to help.

If you are joining us exclusively for Float Trips, let us know and we will provide all trip details and logistics for your reservations.

Contact us directly and we'll take care of the rest.
